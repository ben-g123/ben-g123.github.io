Confirm Your Age For Bugs+

We have suspected that your age is wrong in Bugs+, based on your activity. According to the Bugs+ Rules, setting the wrong age can get you banned, and sometimes into legal trouble. There are quite a lot of options you have, so feel free to have a scroll and choose the best one for the main account user. If you are under 18 years old, ask a parent for help.

Choose a method to correct your age to continue using Bugs+:


Correct Birthdate
We will guide you through the process of changing your birthdate. If the main account user's actual birthdate is eligible for a Kids Account, we will help you set that up as well.

The Birthdate Is Correct

 If your birthdate is correct, you will need to prove it. After proving it, you will less likely to be flagged again for incorrect ages.

Delay This Requirement

 You can delay this requirement for up to 7 days until needing to take action. Failure to do so will result in bans. You will get reminded in 3 days time.

Contact Bugs+ Support

 Bugs+ Support also can help with this age requirement error, so if the other steps don't work, drop us a line!
